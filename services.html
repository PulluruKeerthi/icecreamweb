Ice cream services can offer a variety of services, including:
Manufacturing
Some services manufacture ice cream, gelato, soft ice cream, and frozen yogurt. They may also sell ingredients and accessories.
Customization
Ice cream parlors may offer self-service options that allow customers to customize their ice cream experience. This can include dietary options like vegan ice cream, sorbets, and sauces.
Frozen desserts
Ice cream parlors often serve ice cream in cones, cups, or dishes, but they may also prepare frozen desserts like sundaes, milkshakes, or Boston shakes.
Other services
Some ice cream shops may also offer other services, such as iced coffee, baked goods, or hand-packed pints and quarts.
